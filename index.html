Form Cues
1
Warm up · 5 min
Treadmill walk + arm circles + bodyweight squats. Cold muscles = injury risk.
2
Start light · 50% of working weight
First set of every exercise is a warmup. Don't ego-lift the first set.
3
Form > weight always
If form breaks, lower the weight. Bad reps build bad habits and injuries.
4
Rest 60-90 sec between sets
Longer for compounds (squats, deadlifts), shorter for isolation work.
5
Track your weights
Note weight × reps each session. Aim to add 2.5kg or 1 rep weekly.
6
Stop 1-2 reps before failure
Going to failure every set burns out the nervous system. Recoverable volume wins.
7
Missing equipment? Swap smart
No squat rack → goblet squats. No cable → resistance bands. No lat pulldown → assisted pull-ups.
