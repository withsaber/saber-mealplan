Daily Structure
Recomp Protocol
Daily Schedule
7:00 – 8:00 AM
🍳 Meal 1 — High Protein Breakfast
3 eggs + 150g rice + 100g vegetables. Eat this before or right after you wake up. Don't skip; this primes your metabolism for the day.
~42g protein
10:00 AM – 12:00 PM
💧 Hydration Window
2–3 litres of water throughout the day. Start here. Recomp doesn't work if you're dehydrated.
4:00 – 6:00 PM
🏋️ Gym Session
Weight training. Aim for 45–60 min. Focus on compound movements; squats, bench, rows, deadlifts, overhead press. Progressive overload is the goal.
Every day
Within 30 min post-gym
💪 Whey Shake — VPA Premium WPC
1 scoop in 250ml water. Drink immediately after gym. This is your recovery window; don't delay it beyond 30 minutes.
23g protein · 124 cal
7:00 – 8:00 PM
🍗 Meal 2 — Dinner
Chicken or fish (180–200g) + 150g rice + daal. Reduce chicken by 30–40g on days you had the shake; the shake already covers that protein gap.
~52–59g protein
9:00 PM onward
😴 Wind Down
Sleep is where muscle is actually built. Aim for 7–8 hours. No heavy eating after 9 PM; if hungry, have a glass of water or a small banana.
Weekly Approach
Mon–Fri: Full protocol every day; gym + 2 meals + shake.
Sat: Active rest; light walk or stretching. Eat same meals, skip the shake.
Sun: Full rest. Eat the same meals. Your body repairs on rest days too.

Your Daily Protein Target

How You Hit 126g
🍳 Meal 1 — 3 Eggs
19g
protein
3 whole eggs = ~19g protein. Don't throw the yolk; that's where the healthy fat and micronutrients are. Scramble, boil, or fry; doesn't matter.
🍳 Meal 1 — Rice (150g cooked)
3g
protein + 45g carbs
150g cooked rice = roughly 1 medium bowl. This is your fuel, not your enemy. Recomp needs carbs for energy and muscle glycogen.
💪 Post-Workout Shake
23g
protein
1 scoop (30g) VPA WPC in 250ml water. Drink within 30 minutes of finishing your last set. This is non-negotiable timing.
🍗 Meal 2 — Chicken Breast
45g
protein
150g raw chicken breast (not 180g on shake days). Your palm size = roughly 120–150g. Weigh it once to calibrate your eye; after that you won't need a scale.
🍲 Meal 2 — Masoor Daal (50g dry)
12g
protein + 30g carbs
50g dry = roughly 3 heaped tablespoons before cooking. Expands 2–3x when cooked. This + chicken = a complete amino acid profile.
🐟 Alt: Fish (200g raw)
38g
protein
Swap chicken for fish 2x a week. Rui, Catla, or Tilapia work. Lighter on digestion, still high protein. 200g raw = roughly your full hand size.
